About me
======
I'm currently a junior student at Peking University and major in computer science. In parallel, I'm under my internship in Prof.[Baoquan Chen](http://cfcs.pku.edu.cn/baoquan/)'s lab at PKU. My current areas of interests include application of deep learning in computer graphics, especially video generation. I also had some experience on computer vision, e.g. vehicle detection during my internship at Megvii.

About Computer Graphics
======
From my perspective, computer graphics, as an inverse procedure of computer vision, will become more mixed with computer vision. Analogous to the success in computer vision, deep learning is also widely used in areas of computer graphics. I'd really love to see what will come about with the combination of CV, CG and DL techniques.
