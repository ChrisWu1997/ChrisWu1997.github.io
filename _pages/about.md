About me
======
I'm currently a junior student at Peking University and major in computer science. In parallel, I'm under my internship in VCL lab at PKU, advised by Prof.[Baoquan Chen](http://cfcs.pku.edu.cn/baoquan/). Before that, I worked at Megvii as an intern during the summer of 2018. My current areas of interests include deep learning, computer graphics and computer vision. I enjoy learning and expolring, especially in the middle ground of computer vision and graphics.
